Welcome to the Dr. Rahman's Research Lab

The primary research focuses of the Dr. Rahman's research lab are natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, deep learning, data analytics and child and adolescent mental health. The lab has deep passion for investigating and developing new machine learning and NLP techniques to understand large datasets, such as social media and child health data.

The long-term goal of the lab is to develop new mechanisms and real-world applications in the field of pediatrics, mental health and clinical psychology using advanced NLP and machine learning techniques. The new analytical mechanisms will help translational research scientists including clinical psychologists, social scientist and behavioral therapists. The mechanisms could be extracting information about patients from medical records, predicting important trends regarding mental health of a patient from social interactions and geoinformation, and developing models for identifying mental health disorders.
