Welcome to the ChiDA Lab

The primary research focuses of the ChiDA lab are natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, deep learning, data analytics and child well-being. The lab has deep passion for investigating and developing new machine learning and NLP techniques to understand large datasets, such as social media and child health data.

The long-term goal of the lab is to develop new mechanisms and real-world applications in the field of paediatrics, mental health and clinical psychology using advanced NLP and machine learning techniques. The new analytical mechanisms will help translational research scientists including clinical psychologists, pharmacologists and behavioral therapists. The mechanisms could be extracting information about patients from medical records, predicting important trends regarding mental health of a patient from social interactions and geoinformation, and developing models for identifying mental health disorders.
